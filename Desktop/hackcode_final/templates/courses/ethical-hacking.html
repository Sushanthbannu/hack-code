Introduction to Ethical Hacking

What is Ethical Hacking?

Ethical hacking involves legally breaking into computers and devices to test an organization's defenses. It's among the most exciting careers in cybersecurity. Ethical hackers are hired to identify vulnerabilities before malicious attackers do.

Why Ethical Hacking is Important

With the rise of cyber threats, organizations must proactively defend their systems. Ethical hackers help prevent data breaches, identity theft, and financial loss by uncovering weaknesses and patching them before real attackers strike.

Skills Required

  • Networking Knowledge
  • Understanding of Operating Systems (especially Linux)
  • Programming Skills (Python, Bash, etc.)
  • Familiarity with Security Tools
  • Strong Analytical Thinking

Popular Tools

  • Nmap
  • Wireshark
  • Burp Suite
  • Metasploit
  • John the Ripper
